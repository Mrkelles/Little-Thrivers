# **App Name**: Calgary DayHome Connect

## Core Features:

- Welcome & Home Page: A warm and inviting landing page showcasing the daycare's philosophy and key offerings with an immediate call to action.
- About Us Section: Dedicated section detailing the daycare's nurturing environment, provider's credentials, and educational approach.
- Services & Programs: Comprehensive display of daily schedules, curriculum highlights, and age-specific activities offered at the daycare.
- Photo & Video Gallery: Showcase high-quality images and short videos of the facility, children engaged in play, and learning activities, all featuring organic clip-path treatments.
- Contact & Inquiry Form: An intuitive form for prospective parents to inquire about enrollment, availability, and general questions.
- AI Parent Information Tool: An interactive tool powered by AI to provide instant answers to frequently asked questions about the daycare's policies, fees, or educational methods.
- Location & Hours Display: Clearly presents the daycare's Calgary location with an embedded map, along with detailed operating hours and contact information.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Warm and energetic orange (#F5821F), derived from the design system's accent palette, embodying energy and vibrancy. (HSL: 30, 90%, 54%)
- Background Color: Soft, inviting off-white cream (#FAF5EE) as specified in the design system, ensuring a nurturing and light ambiance. (HSL: 36, 67%, 96%)
- Accent Color: Bold and playful red (#E84040), providing strong contrast and aligning with the design system's secondary accent options for highlights and important calls to action. (HSL: 0, 82%, 57%)
- Secondary accent colors (green #5BBF4E, yellow #F5C518, purple #7B5EA7, and teal #5BC8C0) from the provided palette will be used for decorative elements, section headings, and to provide visual variety.
- Main titles and primary taglines: 'Bubblegum Sans' (display font) for a playful, friendly branding feel. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Section Headings: 'Fredoka One' (display font) for bold and distinct section titles. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Body text: 'Nunito' (sans-serif) for clean, rounded, and highly readable content throughout the site. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Simple, flat, or emoji-style icons, especially for contact information, rendered inline with text and often colored in the accent orange, consistent with the playful yet clear approach.
- Asymmetric, layered, single-column card-like structure with content zones. Photos are clipped into irregular organic brush-stroke shapes and subtly overlap text blocks, avoiding rigid grid layouts to maintain a handcrafted, dynamic feel.
- Subtle, playful entrance animations for content sections and slight hover effects for interactive elements to enhance the energetic and welcoming feel without being distracting.
